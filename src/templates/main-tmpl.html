Bengali Association of Greater Chicago

Celeberating 50 Years of Bengali Culture and Heritage

We are a Chicago based non-profit organization established in 1974. Banga Bhavan, located centrally in Chicago land, is dedicated in promoting literature, art and cultural heritage of Bengal. We are a strong community where we socialize together, celebrate festival together and work together. This website is a medium by which we can continue to enhance and foster out community's togetherness.
